Dear All,

As per the requirement for AI-based meeting documentation, we have incorporated a new AI Meeting Notes tool (Teams Maestro) that can be used during meetings, especially for external meetings.

This tool will automatically join the meeting and generate:

  • AI-based meeting transcripts
  • Short summary of discussions
  • Detailed transcript of the entire meeting

Please go through the attached SOP which includes steps to login, instructions on how to use the tool, and guidelines on how to add it to meetings. Works with Microsoft Teams, Zoom and Google Meet.
